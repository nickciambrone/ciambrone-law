About

For almost 30 years, The Law Office of Francis A. Ciambrone, Esq. has provided outstanding legal service to its clients throughout the State of New Jersey. The firm’s clients range from the largest land developers in the State of New Jersey to first-time homebuyers.

Regardless of the size of the transaction, the Law Office of Francis A Ciambrone, Esq. provides each client with the personal attention so often lacking in today’s business environment. The firm is dedicated to meeting the needs of its clients in all areas of residential and commercial real estate, land use, personal injury, estate planning, and Municipal Court..
