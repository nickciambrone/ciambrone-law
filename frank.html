Francis A. Ciambrone

Frank Ciambrone concentrates in transactional real estate and land use throughout the State of New Jersey. For almost 30 years, Frank has been legal counsel to purchasers and sellers of real estate ranging from residential properties to multi-tenant buildings and malls.

Born and raised in Paramus New Jersey, Frank is the proud father of 4 sons. Together with his wife of 26 years, Adrian, Frank understands the importance of family and maintaining strong relationships. Frank applies those same principles to his practice and has built and maintained relationships with clients that have spanned decades.

Frank began his law career after graduating with honors from Widener University School of Law in Wilmington Delaware and served as law clerk to the Hon. Bruce A. Gaeta, J.S.C.. After working at a law firm where he was elevated to partner, Frank started his own office in Paramus where his practice grew to where it is today.

Frank represents a wide variety of clients and has established himself as one of the premier real estate attorneys in New Jersey. Those who have retained Frank know their case will receive the personal attention it deserves and will benefit from the expertise gleaned by Frank and his support staff who collectively have more than 75 years’ experience in real estate law. Having closed more than 10,000 transactions, Frank’s vast knowledge allows clients to enjoy the success that comes from hard work and dedication.
